About Us

Welcome to Matrubhoomi Mahavidyalaya!

Established in August 2008 by Satish R. Usture, Matrubhoomi Mahavidyalaya is committed to providing quality education in various disciplines. Our college is a beacon of learning, fostering academic excellence and holistic development.

Our Mission

To deliver outstanding education that equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in their respective fields.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that nurtures future leaders, innovators, and professionals.

Affiliation

We are proudly affiliated with Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University (SRTMU), Nanded, ensuring our programs meet high academic standards.

Courses Offered

At Matrubhoomi Mahavidyalaya, we offer undergraduate programs designed to prepare students for successful careers:

  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  • Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS)

Facilities

Our college boasts a range of facilities to support both academic and extracurricular activities:

  • Laboratory: Well-equipped labs for practical learning.
  • Computer Center: State-of-the-art computer labs for IT courses.
  • ICBSE: Dedicated resources for advanced studies.

Campus Location

Matrubhoomi Mahavidyalaya is conveniently located behind the LIC Office on Degloor Road in Udgir, Latur District.
